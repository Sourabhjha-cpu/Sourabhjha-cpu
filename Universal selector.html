KOLKATA KNIGHT RIDERS

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Kolkata in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is owned by Bollywood actor Shahrukh Khan ,actress Juhi Chawla and her spouse Jay Mehta. The Knight Riders Play at the iconic Eden Gardens stadium. The franchise which has gained immense popularity due to its association with celebrity owmers,qualified for the IPL playoffs for the first time in 2011.

They become the IPL champions in 2012, by defending CSK in the Final .They repeated the feat in 2014, defeating KXIP. The Knight Riders hold the record for the longest winning streak by any indian team in T20s (14).

The side's all-time leading run-scorer is "GAUTAM GAMBHIR", while their leading wicket taker is "SUNIL NARINE".
